Olle Andreasson

Creative Staff Engineer

I am a Back-end Staff Engineer located in Gothenburg, Sweden with over 10 years of work experience backed by a CS degree.

But I wouldn't really describe myself as such, because we're all so much more than our professional titles. To categorize is hard and we can go as far as we want down the rabbit hole of who we really are.

When I applied to Gothenburg University in the spring of 2010 I was torn. Because at the same time I also applied for an acting school a bit outside the city. Two completely different paths in life, one creative path and one rational (in a dual sense). As you might understand, I chose the latter.

Luckily, the route we take professionally has no real imprint on what we really are. That's why I call myself a Creative Engineer, and that's something I try to show with this website which I wrote during my parental leave in 2025 in native JavaScript.

Oted Online

The algorithm running in the canvas is similar to Conway's Game of Life with some personal adjustments. The architecture is event driven where one tile can call its neighbours limited by a declining strength of the color it contains.
